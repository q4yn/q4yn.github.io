The Story Behind q4yn

From Digital Signal to Cosmic Sound

q4yn is the electronic music project of producer Alex V., an artist dedicated to exploring the limitless boundaries of sound. The journey began in a small home studio, captivated by old synthesizers and the expansive feeling of deep space. q4yn's music is a unique blend, fusing the heavy synth beats of future bass with the atmospheric, emotional textures of ambient and synthwave.

From the pulsing energy of the 'Starlight EP' to the melodic introspections of earlier work, q4yn invites listeners to turn down the lights, put on their best headphones, and experience music that feels both nostalgic and totally new. The mission is simple: to build a universe, one beat at a time.
